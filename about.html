About Us

Greenlight brings together farmers, agribusinesses, civil society, knowledge institutes and governments. United in diversity, our members show that agribusiness and development are not mutually exclusive. Together, we find new, sustainable ways of creating impact with business. Exchanging perspectives and expecting the unexpected. Cultivating collaboration through linking, learning and leadership.
